Background color #2C2416 is a very dark brown, almost black.

For WCAG AA compliance, we need 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum.

White (#FFFFFF) on #2C2416 should give approximately 16:1 ratio.

At 90% opacity, it's effectively #E6E6E6 which would be around 14:1.
